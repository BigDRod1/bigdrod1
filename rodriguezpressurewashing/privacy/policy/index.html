Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Rodriguez Pressure Washing (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, and the choices you have. This page is provided for your use with Meta (Facebook and Instagram) business tools, including lead forms and messaging, where a privacy policy URL may be required.

Who we are

Rodriguez Pressure Washing is a small business offering pressure washing services. For questions about this policy or your information, contact us using the details in the Contact section below.

Information we collect

When you interact with us through Meta lead ads, contact forms, or similar, we may collect:

  • Name — so we can address you and confirm who requested service.
  • Phone number — so we can call or text you to discuss your request, provide estimates, and schedule work.

We only collect what you voluntarily submit or what is needed to respond to your inquiry. We do not use this page to collect sensitive categories of data (such as health or financial account numbers) unless you choose to share that information in a message, in which case we use it only to respond to you.

How we use your information

We use your name and phone number to:

  • Respond to your questions and quote requests;
  • Schedule and perform pressure washing services you ask for;
  • Contact you about your service (for example, arrival time or follow-up);
  • Comply with law or protect our rights when necessary.

We do not sell your personal information.

Legal bases (for example, if you are in the EEA or UK)

If applicable data protection laws require a “legal basis,” we rely on your consent (when you submit a form through Meta or our site) and/or our legitimate interest in responding to leads and operating our business, balanced against your rights.

Sharing of information

We may share your information only as needed:

  • Service providers — for example, phone/SMS providers or scheduling tools that help us run the business, under strict terms that limit use to providing services to us.
  • Meta platforms — data you submit through Facebook or Instagram is processed under Meta’s terms and policies as well as this policy.
  • Legal requirements — if we are required to disclose information by law or to protect safety and rights.

Retention

We keep your contact details only as long as needed to respond to your request, complete services, and maintain ordinary business records (for example, for customer service or tax purposes). When information is no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it in line with our record-keeping practices.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, but we take steps appropriate to the nature of the data we handle.

Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to:

  • Access or correct the personal information we hold about you;
  • Request deletion of your information, subject to legal exceptions;
  • Object to or restrict certain processing;
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below. If you are in the EU or UK, you may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

U.S. state privacy disclosures (e.g., California)

If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, to delete certain information, and to opt out of “sale” or “sharing” of personal information. We do not sell your personal information in the conventional sense. We do not knowingly collect or sell personal information of minors under 16. To submit a request, use the contact details below.

Children

Our services are not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of our services after changes means you accept the updated policy.
